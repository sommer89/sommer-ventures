Football Owner

A football club ownership game set in the English league pyramid. You are not the manager picking the starting eleven — you are the owner who hired him, and who has to live with the consequences.

You start at the bottom, in the National League, with a club that has more problems than money. From there it is your job to appoint the right manager and sack him when it goes wrong, fund the transfers, decide whether the money goes into the stadium or the academy, and steer the club through the long climb toward the Premier League.

Seasons play out around your decisions, and the league does not wait for you to be ready. The whole game runs on a deterministic simulation engine, so results follow consistently from the state of your club rather than from arbitrary dice rolls.

It plays entirely offline, on device. No account, no sign-in, no internet connection required.
