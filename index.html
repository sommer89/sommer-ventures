We invest for the long term — and build things ourselves.

Sommer Ventures ApS is an independent investment and holding company in Denmark. We put our own capital to work across markets, property and technology, and we build software of our own alongside it.

The company

Sommer Ventures ApS is privately owned and funded entirely with its own capital. We answer to no outside investors and we take no client work, which means we can hold a position for as long as it deserves to be held, and walk away from anything that does not.

We are generalists by choice. A good investment can be a listed company, a building, or an idea that does not exist yet — and we would rather judge each on its merits than confine ourselves to one asset class.

What we do

Invest

Long-term positions across public markets, property and private companies. Our own capital, our own timeline, no fund to answer to.

Explore

Ideas worth a look, before anyone can tell whether they are businesses. Some become products, some stay experiments, and we are content with that.

Build

Software we design, develop and publish ourselves — and support ourselves after release. The apps below are what that currently looks like.
